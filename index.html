My Home Ankura 4 BHK Luxury Triplex villas at Tellapur

My Home Ankura is a luxury triplex villa project located at Tellapur, Hyderabad. The project is spread over 12.5 acres of land and has 162 units. The project offers 4 BHK villas with a built-up area of 4,000 sq. ft. to 4,500 sq. ft. The project is approved by HMDA and is in the construction stage. The project is expected to be completed by December 2023. The project offers various amenities such as a swimming pool, gymnasium, children's play area, clubhouse, multipurpose hall, and many more.
